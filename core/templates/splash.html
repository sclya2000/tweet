Welcome to "Twitter"

This is my implementation of a Twitter clone. You can do things like sign up, sign in, and log out of accounts. You can also view posts with specific hashtags and like posts. The limitations for posts and hashtags are the same as on Twitter-- "Tweets" have a 280 character limit and hashtags have a 18 character limit. Click on the links below to explore the site!
